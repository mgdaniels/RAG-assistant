# Procurement and Purchasing Policy

*Owner: Finance Team (Elena Vasquez) — last updated 15 January 2026*

How to buy goods and services on behalf of Northwind.

## Approval thresholds

Approval depends on the total value of the purchase:

| Purchase value | Approval required |
| --- | --- |
| Up to £250 | Line manager |
| £250 – £2,000 | Department head |
| Over £2,000 | Department head **and** Head of Finance sign-off |

These thresholds apply to the **total** cost, including a full year of any subscription — don't split a purchase to stay under a limit.

## Raising a purchase order

Always **raise a purchase order (PO) in ExpenseDesk before you commit to the spend**. We do not accept retrospective POs, and suppliers are asked to quote a valid PO number on their invoices.

## Preferred suppliers

Finance maintains a **preferred-supplier list** in ExpenseDesk. Use a preferred supplier where one exists; if you need a new supplier, Finance will run the onboarding and checks.

## Software and SaaS

Any **software or SaaS purchase requires an IT security review before purchase, regardless of value** (see IT Onboarding and the Information Security Policy). This is to protect customer data and avoid duplicate tools.

## Questions

Contact **finance@northwind.example** before purchasing if you're unsure which threshold applies.
