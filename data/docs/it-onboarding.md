# IT Onboarding and Equipment

*Owner: IT Team (Raj Patel) — last updated 15 January 2026*

Everything you need to get set up, and how to get help.

## Your equipment

Every new starter chooses a laptop: a **MacBook Air (Apple silicon)** or a **Lenovo ThinkPad**. IT provisions it before your first day. Your manager will tell IT which peripherals your role needs (extra monitor, dock, headset).

## Accounts and access

Your accounts are created in **Northwind SSO** before you start, giving you single sign-on to Slack, Google Workspace, Jira, and Confluence. You'll set your password and register multi-factor authentication on day one — see the Information Security Policy for the rules.

## Getting help

Contact the IT helpdesk at **it-help@northwind.example** or in Slack at **`#it-help`**.

| Request type | Target response |
| --- | --- |
| Standard request | Within 1 business day |
| Urgent (cannot work) | Within 2 hours |

## Requesting software

Request new software or SaaS tools through the **IT Service Portal**. Anything that will handle **customer data** must pass an **IT security review** before purchase — this is also a requirement of the Procurement Policy, regardless of cost.

## Returning equipment

If you leave Northwind, return all company equipment to IT on or before your last day. Lost or stolen devices must be reported immediately — see the Information Security Policy for the one-hour reporting rule.
