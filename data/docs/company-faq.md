# Northwind Robotics — Company FAQ

*Owner: People Team — last updated 15 January 2026*

A quick reference for new joiners and a "who do I ask?" guide for everyone.

## About the company

Northwind Robotics Ltd was founded in **2017** and is headquartered in **Bristol, UK**, with a second office in **Manchester**. We are around **180 people**.

We build autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) for warehouses and distribution centres. Our two products are **Drift**, our fleet of warehouse robots, and **Beacon**, the software that plans and manages a Drift fleet. Our mission is to make warehouse automation safe and accessible.

Our company values are **Safety, Curiosity, Candour, and Ownership**.
