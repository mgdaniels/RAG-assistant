# Benefits Summary

*Owner: People Team — last updated 15 January 2026*

An overview of what Northwind offers beyond salary. For full terms, see PeopleHub or contact people@northwind.example.

## Pension

You're automatically enrolled in our workplace pension. If you contribute **5% of your salary, Northwind matches it with 5%** (employer contribution up to 5%).

## Health and wellbeing

- **Private medical insurance** through **WellCover**, available from your first day. You can add family members at cost.
- **Life assurance** worth **4× your annual salary**.
- A **24/7 Employee Assistance Programme** (counselling and support) included with WellCover.

## Time off and family

- **25 days annual leave** plus 8 bank holidays (see the Annual Leave Policy).
- **Enhanced parental leave**: 16 weeks at full pay for the primary carer, 4 weeks at full pay for the secondary carer.
- **2 paid volunteering days** per year.

## Learning and development

- A **£500 personal learning and development budget** each year for courses, books, and subscriptions.
- Support to attend one relevant conference per year, subject to manager approval.

## Everyday perks

- **Cycle-to-work scheme** and **season-ticket loans**.
- Free tea, coffee, and fruit in both offices.

Claims related to these benefits (such as the L&D budget) go through ExpenseDesk with manager approval.
