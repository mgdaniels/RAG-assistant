# Expenses and Travel Policy

*Owner: Finance Team — last updated 15 January 2026*

How to claim work expenses and book business travel.

## Claiming expenses

Submit all claims through **ExpenseDesk** within **30 days** of the spend. A **receipt is required for any item over £10**. Your line manager approves claims; any claim **over £500** also requires department-head approval.

Approved expenses are reimbursed with your salary on the **last working day of the month**, paid into the same account as your salary.

## What you can claim

| Category | Limit |
| --- | --- |
| Mileage (own car) | 45p per mile for the first 10,000 miles, 25p thereafter |
| Meals while travelling (UK) | Up to £30 per day |
| Meals while travelling (international) | Up to £45 per day |
| Client entertainment | Up to £50 per head, pre-approved by a department head |

Alcohol is only reimbursable as part of approved client entertainment.

## Booking travel

Book your own travel and claim it back, or ask Finance to book larger trips.

- **Rail:** standard class by default. First class is only permitted for journeys **longer than 5 hours**, with manager approval.
- **Flights:** economy by default for flights under 6 hours.
- **Hotels:** book a reasonable mid-range hotel; check the preferred-supplier list in ExpenseDesk first.

## Questions

For anything unclear, contact **finance@northwind.example** before you spend, not after.
