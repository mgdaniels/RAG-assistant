# Annual Leave and Sickness Policy

*Owner: People Team — last updated 15 January 2026*

This policy covers paid annual leave, public holidays, sickness, and parental leave for all permanent employees.

## Annual leave

Full-time employees receive **25 days of paid annual leave** per year, **in addition to the 8 UK public (bank) holidays** — a total of 33 days off. The leave year runs from **1 January to 31 December**.

Leave accrues monthly. In your first year, your allowance is **pro-rated** from your start date. Part-time employees receive a pro-rated allowance based on contracted hours.

You may **carry over up to 5 unused days** into the next leave year, and these must be taken by **31 March**. Any balance above 5 days is lost at year end, so plan ahead.

## Booking leave

Book all leave through **PeopleHub**, our HR system. Your line manager approves requests.

- For **5 or more consecutive days**, give at least **two weeks' notice**.
- For shorter requests, give as much notice as you reasonably can.

## Sickness

If you are unwell and cannot work, **notify your line manager by 9:30 am** on the first day, ideally by phone or Slack, and log it in PeopleHub.

- You may **self-certify for up to 7 calendar days**.
- For absences **longer than 7 days**, you must provide a fit note from your GP.

Northwind provides **company sick pay at full salary for up to 10 working days** per leave year, after which Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) applies.

## Parental leave

We offer enhanced parental leave: **16 weeks at full pay** for the primary carer and **4 weeks at full pay** for the secondary carer, on top of statutory entitlements. See the Benefits Summary for details and contact people@northwind.example to plan your leave.
