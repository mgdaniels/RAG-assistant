# Code of Conduct

*Owner: People Team — last updated 15 January 2026*

How we treat each other and the standards we hold ourselves to. This applies to everyone at Northwind, on and off site, and at company events.

## Our standards

We live our values — **Safety, Curiosity, Candour, and Ownership** — in how we work together. In practice that means:

- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect.
- We have **zero tolerance for harassment, bullying, or discrimination** of any kind.
- We are an equal-opportunity employer and value a diverse, inclusive workplace.
- Give and receive feedback honestly and kindly (that's Candour).

## Raising a concern

If something doesn't feel right, you have several routes:

1. Talk to your **line manager**.
2. Contact the **People team** at people@northwind.example.
3. Use the **SpeakUp line**, our confidential and anonymous reporting channel, for anything you'd rather raise outside the management chain.

All concerns are taken seriously and handled confidentially. **Whistleblowers are protected** — no one will face retaliation for raising a genuine concern in good faith.

## Conflicts of interest

Disclose any potential conflict of interest (for example, a close relationship with a supplier or candidate) to your manager and the People team.

## Gifts and hospitality

You may accept small tokens of appreciation, but **decline any gift worth more than £50** and disclose it to the People team. Never accept gifts, cash, or hospitality intended to influence a business decision.
